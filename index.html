Computer Science-Engg

The Department of Computer Science is dedicated to the study and advancement of the principles, theory, and application of computer systems. The department offers a wide variety of courses and research opportunities in areas like algorithms, programming languages, software engineering, artificial intelligence, data science, computer networks, cybersecurity, databases, machine learning, human-computer interaction, and more.

department of

Information Technology-Engg

The Department of Information Technology (IT) is a specialized academic or professional department that focuses on the study, application, and advancement of information technology. This field encompasses the development, management, and use of computer systems, software, and networks to process, store, and exchange information. IT plays a crucial role in almost every aspect of modern society, from business operations to healthcare, education, and entertainment.

Mechanical-Engg

Mechanical Engineering is one of the oldest and most versatile branches of engineering. It focuses on the design, analysis, manufacturing, and maintenance of mechanical systems. Mechanical engineers work with principles of physics, mathematics, and material science to create machines, devices, and systems that solve real-world problems.
